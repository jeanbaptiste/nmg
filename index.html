Nomadic Research Lab

At the intersection of exploration and invention, the Nomadic Research Lab is creating technologies that thrive in a world with new constraints. We believe in the spirit of distributed presence, the wisdom of nomadic cultures, and the potential of learning and creating in the wild.

Wild Tech

We build untethered and decentralized technologies for a world in motion. Our work on mesh networks, wearable computing, and deep presence is celebrating the resilience and ingenuity of nomadic cultures, enabling connection in any environment.

Featured Projects & Concepts:

  • Disposable Mesh Networks
  • UnWearable Presence
  • Deep Presence

Situated Learning & Creativity

True understanding comes from doing. We design tools and experiences that take learning out of the classroom and into the wild, fostering playful exploration and collaborative discovery through direct interaction with the world.

Featured Projects & Concepts:

  • Ad-Hoc Learning Environments
  • High-Low Tech Tangible Interfaces & DIY Kits
  • Dynamic Creative Modeling on the Go

Dynamic Biomaterials

The materials of the future will be alive, adaptable, and sentient We explore and create programmable biomaterials that bridge the living world and the digital realm, from bio-compatible electronics to situated bio-composites.

Featured Projects & Concepts:

  • Mineral and Inorganic Polymers
  • Functionalized Natural Environments
  • Biocompatible Responsive Materials
